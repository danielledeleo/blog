```
Not marching
Not floating
Not frozen
Nor rushing

It's steady and stable
But not a gallop or trot

It flaps and it glides
It can sustain, and it does

There's no guilt in a pause
You land on a perch
To breathe and swoop
And resume calmly

Momentum and potential
```
